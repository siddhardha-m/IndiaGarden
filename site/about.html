India Garden Restaurant

How we opened

The owner of India Garden restaurant, Rakesh K., has worked in the Indian restaurant industry with his family for the past 15 years. He has always dreamt of opening his own restaurant. Many people suggested Rakesh to open a new Indian restaurant in Bloomington, IN. Rakesh soon got the chance in 2013, when he opened India Garden in Bloomington.

Established in 2013, India Garden in Bloomington is just getting started. Look out for new deals, dishes, and events in the weeks and months ahead.

Meet the owner

Rakesh K., owner of India Garden restaurant, was born in India and came to the US in 1996 to help with his family's restaurant in Indianapolis, IN. He worked in the industry for 15 years, with the ultimate dream of starting an Indian restaurant of his own. That time finally came when he had the opertunity to purchase Amole India. Rakesh used his life savings to purchase it and start his restaurant in Bloomington, IN. His wife and three children still live in Indianapolis, so he commutes every other day to spend time with them. Rakesh hopes to move them down to Bloomington once India Garden is well established.
